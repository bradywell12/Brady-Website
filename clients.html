Import LinkedIn Connections

How to export your LinkedIn connections:

  1. Go to LinkedIn.com and click your profile picture
  2. Go to Settings & Privacy
  3. Click Data Privacy → Get a copy of your data
  4. Select "Connections" and click Request Archive
  5. LinkedIn will email you a download link (usually within 10 minutes)
  6. Download and unzip the file — find Connections.csv
  7. Upload that file below
Tip: LinkedIn's CSV includes First Name, Last Name, Email Address, Company, and Position. All available fields will be imported automatically.
